About Me

I’m Stephanie Harris and I’ve recently left a career as a church music director to become a web developer. I actually built my very first website when I was 11, but it wasn’t until last year, when I signed up for an online coding bootcamp, that I seriously considered a career in tech. I still love music, but I want a more rewarding career that allows me to continuously learn new things.


I knew that I would benefit more, and progress quicker, by participating in the more collaborative and immersive experience that an in person bootcamp like Rutgers’ offers. So I switched to Rutgers’ Coding Bootcamp where I’ve developed skills in many front and back-end technologies like the ones listed below.


For more about me, check out my Project Showcase, Blog, or the Portfolio that I built right before I started at Rutgers.
