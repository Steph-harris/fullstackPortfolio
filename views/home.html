About Me

I’m Stephanie Harris and I’ve recently left a career as a church music director (CLICK HERE) to become a web developer. I actually built my very first website when I was 11, but it wasn’t until 2015, when I signed up for an online coding bootcamp, that I seriously considered a career in tech.
